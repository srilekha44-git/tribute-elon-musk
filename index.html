Elon Musk has been recognized globally for his innovative contributions and has received numerous awards:

About Elon Musk

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He is the founder and CEO of SpaceX, Tesla, Neuralink, and The Boring Company.
